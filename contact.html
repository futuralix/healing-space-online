Frequently Asked Questions

How long are therapy sessions?

Standard therapy sessions are 50 minutes long. Initial consultations may be scheduled for 60-90 minutes to allow for a comprehensive assessment.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes, we offer secure telehealth sessions for your convenience. Virtual sessions are just as effective as in-person therapy for most concerns.

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

If you're feeling overwhelmed, struggling with relationships, or simply want to improve your mental well-being, therapy can help. Schedule a consultation to discuss your specific needs.

Is everything I share confidential?

Yes, therapy is completely confidential except in rare situations involving immediate danger to yourself or others, or legally mandated reporting requirements.
